Family

Skiing is our favorite thing to do as a family, since both my parents and siblings grew up ski racing. My sister and I grew up racing as well, and we love watching the U.S ski team in the World Cup and Olympics. My sister and I grew up watching Mikaela Shiffrin, who is now the most decorated American alpine skier as of recent.
